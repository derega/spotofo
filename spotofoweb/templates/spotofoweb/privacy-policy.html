Spotofo knows about you:

  • Your Spotify profile
  • What you are listening now on Spotify
  • What devices you have connected on your Spotify account

Spotofo stores all information listed above in a database, and the information is also available to other users of the service, and may be made publicly available.

Spotofo does not have your password, only your oAuth tokens to Spotify. With these tokens Spotofo, or someone who gets access to those tokens, can use Spotify API to the extent you have granted access for Spotofo.

Spotofo uses cookies to track your open session.
